What is Vermicompost?

Vermicompost is a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer produced through the natural decomposition of organic matter by earthworms. This "black gold" of agriculture is created when earthworms consume organic waste and transform it into a premium soil amendment packed with essential nutrients, beneficial microorganisms, and plant growth hormones.

Unlike chemical fertilizers that can harm soil health over time, vermicompost enriches the soil structure, improves water retention, enhances microbial activity, and provides slow-release nutrients that feed plants naturally throughout their growth cycle.
